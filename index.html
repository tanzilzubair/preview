About This Course

MI 488 / IAH 205 merges HCI with African studies, exploring how technology is used and designed, primarily in the Kenyan context. throughout the course, students collaborated virtually with Technologists-in-Residence from Egerton University, applying HCD to address global challenges. They developed prototypes with Kenyan designers and showcased their projects at a campus-wide event, supported by funding from the Mozilla Foundationâ€™s Responsible Computing Project
