MI 488: Integrating African Perspectives into Technology and Design

In collaboration with the Mozilla Foundation

About This Course

MI 488 merges HCI with African studies, exploring how technology is used and designed, primarily in the Kenyan context. throughout the course, students collaborated virtually with Technologists-in-Residence from Egerton University, applying HCD to address global challenges. They developed prototypes with Kenyan designers and showcased their projects at a campus-wide event, supported by funding from the Mozilla Foundation’s Responsible Computing Program .
