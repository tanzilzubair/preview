A combined hardware and software approach to better monitor and manage energy consumption effectively in Kenyan communities.

About

This project presents a combined hardware and software approach to allow Kenyan communities with less reliable access to electricity to better monitor and manage their consumption. It also aims to givethem the tools to increase the efficiency of the energy economy of their households and communities, through AI based technologies.

The project also aims to integrate more withe stablished Kenyan infrastructure, with plans to allow incorporating locally generated solar power and energy supplied from the National Grid into a cohesive singular system, aiming to meet and manage all of the energy needs that may arise in these households.
