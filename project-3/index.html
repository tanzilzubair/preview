About

This project aims to be a gateway to Kenyan culture, and a hub for all the sounds that make it up. Innovating on current music and audio distribution technologies to both preserve the work of past artists, and provide a launchpad for budding newcomers. From incorporating biographies and discographies to allow their stories to be heard alongside their music, to integrating VR technologies to provide live concert experiences to a huge potential audience, Benga Archive strives to not just be a streaming platform for music, but to drive Kenyan culture forward.
