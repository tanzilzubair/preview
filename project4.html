About

Shewell is a mobile app platform aiming to provide a range of personalized services to support and enhance maternal healthcare, including period tracking, ovulation prediction, symptom logging, individualized health insights. It also provides access to educational resources on menstrual health, fertility, and overall wellbeing.

It aims to transform access to maternal healthcare, particularly as it relates to rural Kenyan communities, where a significant portion of women face difficulties accessing essential reproductive health services.
